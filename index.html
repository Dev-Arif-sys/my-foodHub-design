About Us

Started as an online cookbook where professional Chefs share their favorite recipes, Food Hub has become a valuable resource for professional chef recipes, professional cooking advice, and news from the culinary industry. Food Hub was started by Sydney-based Chef Paul Keylin. In addition to recipes, our featured Chefs also share culinary techniques, advice on cooking materials, and lots of tips for home cooks.

Anticuchos

Anticuchos are skewers of meat or grilled beef heart. Although they don’t sound very interesting and much less delicious, the truth is actually the opposite. This dish can be found in both luxury restaurants and on street stalls.

Orange Cardamon Ricotta Figs

My recent food haul Waitrose & Partners included these gorgeous figs which I stuffed with ricotta, orange, cardamom and pistachios creating the perfect summer dessert or appetizer.

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a popular Filipino dish in which the slices of pork meat is cooked with coconut milk, shrimp paste, and chili pepper. There’s a belief that this dish was adapted from a Bicolano recipe called “Gulay na may Lada,” meaning ‘vegetable with chili.’
